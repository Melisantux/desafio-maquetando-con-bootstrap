Asian Black Bear

The Asian black bear, also known as the Asiatic black bear, moon bear and white-chested bear, is a medium-sized bear species native to Asia that is largely adapted to an arboreal lifestyle.

Brown Bear

The brown bear is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. It is one of the largest living terrestrial members of the order Carnivora, rivaled in size only by the polar bear.

North American Black Bear

The American black bear is a medium-sized bear endemic to North America. It is the continent's smallest and most widely distributed bear species. American black bears are omnivores.

Panda

The panda is a species endemic to China. It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. Is a folivore, with bamboo shoots and leaves making up more than 99% of its diet.

Polar Bear

The polar bear is a hypercarnivorous bear whose native range lies largely within the Arctic Circle, encompassing the Arctic Ocean, its surrounding seas and surrounding land masses.

Sloth Bear

The sloth bear is a species native to the Indian subcontinent. It feeds on fruits, ants and termites. It has also been called "labiated bear" because of its long lower lip used for sucking up insects.

Spectacled Bear

The spectacled bear is a species native to the Andes Mountains in western South America. It is the only living species of bear native to South America, and the last remaining short-faced bear.
